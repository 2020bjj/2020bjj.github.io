WHO ARE WE?

Toronto BJJ is Toronto's most highly accredited Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
and Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) academy. We have been home to several
Brazilian, Canadian, and World champions in both Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and MMA.
Toronto BJJ runs world class programs in BJJ, MMA, and Muay Thai kickboxing.

WHY TRAIN AT TORONTO BJJ?


Friendly atmosphere

We are a team and a family, a place where people of all ages and levels can train.

Amazing instructors

Our instructors have been sought out by everyone, from complete beginners to world champions in the UFC, BJJ, and Muay Thai.

A huge class schedule

We have classes in the morning, afternoon, and evenings. You will surely be able to find classes that fit your schedule.
