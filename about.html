About Toronto BJJ

Toronto BJJ is Toronto's most highly accredited Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) academy. We have been home to several World, Brazilian, and Canadian national champions in both Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Mixed Martial Arts (MMA). Toronto BJJ runs world class programs in BJJ, MMA, and Muay Thai kickboxing.

Our instructors include a Saulo Ribeiro & Royler Gracie Black Belt Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu instructor, a former Canadian Muay Thai kick boxing champion, and we have helped train a K-1 kickboxer, multiple MMA champions, multiple BJJ world Champions Chute Boxe Muay Thai Black Belts, and a UFC champion. Our 9000 square FT + facility features: change rooms, lockers, showers, AC, pro shop, a striking dedicated room with a removable ring, pads, heavy bags, for Muay Thai Kick Boxing and Mixed Martial Arts (MMA), Octagonal Cage, and over 4000 square ft of mats.

Due to our unique location and commitment to excellence we draw many students from throughout Toronto and the GTA. We are located on Bloor Street West, one minute walk west on Bloor from the Christie Subway Station. Proudly located in downtown Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
